Powers and Roots Worksheet

Use squares, cubes, square roots, cube roots, and simple index notation.

Practice questions

  1. Write three things you remember from the lesson.
  2. Solve one easy example and show every step.
  3. Create your own question for this topic.
  4. Answer your own question and check it.
  5. Explain the method to someone else in two sentences.

Answer key / teacher notes

Answers depend on the examples the learner creates. Check that the working is clear, the method matches the topic, and the final answer is sensible.
