How the quiz unlock works

  1. Open the lesson.
  2. Read the Big Idea, Method Ladder, and worked example.
  3. Press Start 10-question quiz.
  4. Question 2 stays locked until Question 1 is correct.
  5. After Question 10, the next mission unlocks.
